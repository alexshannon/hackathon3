Ocean Concervation Network

The ocean covers almost three-quarters of the Earth's surface. It is the world's largest and most powerful producer of oxygen, and the most important source of biological nitrogen.

One of the most biodiverse ecosystem is California Kelp Ecosystem. Numerous species of fish, birds, mammals, and invertebrates call the kelp forest their home.

Between 2014 and now, the California Kelp Ecosystem has been reduced by more than 95%.

We have chosen to highlight three organizations that are working to restore, explore, and preserve the kelp ecosystem.

  • Global Underwater Explorers
  • Marine Science Institute
  • Reef Check
